üí° Assessment Tips & Best Practices

üìù Before Assessment:

  • Ensure quiet, comfortable testing environment
  • Have all materials ready (books, recording sheets)
  • Review student's previous assessment data
  • Plan for 15-20 minutes per student

üìä During Assessment:

  • Start at student's instructional level
  • Take detailed notes on reading behaviors
  • Record accuracy, fluency, and comprehension
  • Stop when student reaches frustration level

üìà After Assessment:

  • Input data immediately while fresh
  • Upload to Google Sheets for tracking
  • Plan instruction based on results
  • Share results with families
